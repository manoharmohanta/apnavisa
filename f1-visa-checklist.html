Documents Required for an F-1 student visa

Your journey to obtaining a visa begins here. At ApnaVisa, we understand the importance of a smooth and stress-free visa application process.

F-1 student visa

  • Original I-20, Approved and signed by student and the University.
  • Sevis Fee Receipt

    Pay the I-901 Student and Exchange Visitor Information System (SEVIS) fee, which includes the cost of the computer system used to record your stay in the U.S.

  • Passport

    Valid passport with at least six months of validity after entry into the U.S

  • DS-160 Form Confirmation Printout.

    Online application for non-immigrant visa that informs the U.S. government you're accepted into an accredited university program and meet all the requirements to study in the U.S.

  • Proof of financial support

    Bank statements, Fixed Deposits, Scholarship letters, Income Tax returns / Form16, Salary Payslips, statement from certified CA, or other financial records.

  • Evidence of English proficiency and Academic tests.

    GRE/GMAT/SAT/TOEFL / IELTS / Duolingo / PTE Score reports

  • Academic transcripts

    Original Marksheets, Diploma or Degree certificates

  • Other Admit Cards
  • Work Experience Documents if Any.

    For employed students: Work experience letters, Appointment Letters, Payslips/salary certificates for the last six months, Form-16 for last 2 years.
