F1 and F2 visas

F1 Visa

The F-1 visa is specifically for students who intend to enrol in a full-time academic program or English language program at a U.S. college, university, seminary, conservatory, academic high school, elementary school, or other academic institution. It allows individuals to enter the United States for the primary purpose of studying.

F2 Visa

The F-2 visa is designed for the dependents of F-1 visa holders who are pursuing academic studies or language training programs in the United States. Dependents eligible for an F-2 visa include the spouse and unmarried children under the age of 21 of the F-1 visa holder.
